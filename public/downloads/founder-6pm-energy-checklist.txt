Founders Quick-Start Guide to 6 PM Energy
=========================================

Use this 10-minute reset to stabilize evening energy.

Checklist:
1) Lock a wake-time anchor (5 days per week).
2) Get outdoor light within 30 minutes of waking.
3) Protein-first lunch: 40g protein plus fiber.
4) Caffeine cutoff by 1:00 PM.
5) 10-minute walk after lunch.
6) Keep carbs in one window (post-training or dinner).
7) Schedule a 15-minute midday reset (walk or breathing).
8) Light dinner, finish 2 hours before bed.
9) In bed 8+ hours before wake time.
10) Set a shutdown cue 60 minutes before bed.
